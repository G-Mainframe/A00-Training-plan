              LABEL RECORD IS STANDARD.

                OUTPUT OUTPUT-FILE.

           MOVE NAME TO OUTLINE.
           WRITE OUTPUT-RECORD AFTER 2.
           GO TO B.
       C.  CLOSE INPUT-FILE  OUTPUT-FILE.
           STOP RUN.
